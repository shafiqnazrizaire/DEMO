About Us

Our Story

Established in 1998, our tour and travel business in Batu Ferringhi (Penang's Bali Escape). We offer comprehensive services for both local and international travelers. With deep local expertise, we provide tailored travel packages at highly competitive rates. We offer car rentals, guided tours, ticket bookings, and adventure activity arrangements, ensuring a seamless travel experience. Known for our trusted reputation in the industry, we are committed to delivering exceptional service and memorable trips.

Our Mission

We believe that travel has the power to transform lives. Our mission is to provide exceptional travel experiences that inspire, educate, and create lasting memories. We're committed to sustainable tourism and supporting local communities.
